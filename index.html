About us

Care that feels personal, capable, and close to nature.

Paw Power K9 Adventure Camp is owned by Victoria, a dog lover since the day she was born. Her years of experience caring for dogs professionally as a vet tech, along with her background in training and lifelong love for dogs, shape every camp day.

Victoria created Paw Power K9 for families who want more than a quick walk or crowded day care. Every outing is built around trust: getting to know each dog, honoring their pace, and helping them come home fulfilled.
